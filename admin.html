University Administration

Office of the Vice Chancellor

The Office of the Vice Chancellor provides strategic leadership and direction for the university. The VC is responsible for the overall administration of the university and the implementation of policies and decisions of the University Council.

Key functions include academic leadership, financial management, human resource development, and maintaining relationships with stakeholders.

Finance Department

The Finance Department manages all financial matters of the university including budgeting, accounting, financial reporting, and procurement.

Our services include student fee management, staff payroll processing, financial planning, and ensuring compliance with financial regulations.
