About the Faculty

Established in 1985, the Faculty of Social Sciences has grown to become one of the most vibrant and diverse faculties at Softcore University. We offer programs that examine all aspects of human society, from individual behavior to global systems.

Our faculty comprises renowned scholars and practitioners who are committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement. We emphasize critical thinking, research skills, and practical applications of social science knowledge.

Dean's Message

"The Faculty of Social Sciences prepares students to understand and address complex social issues. Our graduates leave with the analytical tools and practical skills to make meaningful contributions to society."

Prof. Wanjiku Mwangi
Dean, Faculty of Social Sciences

Departments

Department of Sociology

Examines social behavior, social institutions, and social change through theoretical and empirical approaches.

Department of Psychology

Studies human behavior and mental processes through scientific research and clinical practice.

Department of Political Science

Explores political systems, theories, and international relations at local and global levels.

Department of Economics

Analyzes production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services in societies.

Department of Anthropology

Investigates human cultures, biological evolution, and archaeological remains.

Department of Communication Studies

Focuses on human communication processes across various media and contexts.

Department of Public Policy

Examines the development and implementation of government policies.

Research & Innovation

The Faculty of Social Sciences is at the forefront of research addressing critical social issues. Our faculty members and students engage in interdisciplinary research projects with local and international partners.

Research Focus Areas

Social Inequality

Examining the causes and consequences of social stratification and developing policy solutions.

Mental Health

Investigating psychological well-being and developing community-based interventions.

Governance & Democracy

Analyzing political systems and democratic processes in Africa.

Economic Development

Studying patterns of economic growth and poverty reduction strategies.

Media & Society

Exploring the impact of digital media on social behavior and culture.

Urban Studies

Researching urbanization trends and sustainable city development.
