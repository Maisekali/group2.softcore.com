About the Faculty

The Faculty of Engineering at Softcore University is committed to excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation. Established in 1990, we have grown to become one of the leading engineering schools in the region, with state-of-the-art facilities and strong industry partnerships.

Our programs are designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to address real-world engineering challenges. We emphasize hands-on learning through laboratory work, industrial attachments, and research projects.

Dean's Message

"Our faculty is dedicated to producing engineers who are not only technically competent but also innovative and socially responsible. We foster creativity and problem-solving skills that prepare our graduates for leadership roles in industry and academia."

Prof. Brian Maisiba
Dean, Faculty of Engineering

Engineering Departments

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Focuses on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems. Areas of specialization include thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and machine design.

Department of Civil Engineering

Deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects like buildings, roads, bridges, and water supply systems.

Department of Electrical Engineering

Covers electrical systems, electronics, and power generation with specializations in power systems, control systems, and telecommunications.

Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering

Combines electrical engineering with electronic systems, focusing on circuits, digital systems, and embedded systems design.

Department of Electrical and Communication Engineering

Specializes in communication systems, signal processing, and networking technologies.
