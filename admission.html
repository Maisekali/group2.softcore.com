Admission Requirements

Admission to Softcore University is based on academic qualifications and other criteria as specified for each program. Below are the general requirements:

Undergraduate Programs

  • KCSE certificate with a mean grade of C+ and above or equivalent
  • Minimum subject requirements as specified for each program
  • Completed application form with all required documents
  • Application fee payment receipt
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of national ID or passport

Postgraduate Programs

  • Relevant bachelor's degree with at least Second Class Honors (Upper Division) or equivalent
  • Professional qualifications where applicable
  • Completed application form with all required documents
  • Academic transcripts
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Curriculum Vitae
  • Research proposal (for research-based programs)

Diploma & Certificate Programs

  • KCSE certificate with a mean grade of C- and above or equivalent
  • Completed application form with all required documents
  • Application fee payment receipt
  • Copy of national ID or passport

International Students

International applicants must meet equivalent qualifications and provide:

  • Certified copies of academic certificates and transcripts
  • Evidence of English language proficiency (where applicable)
  • Valid student visa/passport
  • Medical insurance cover

How to Apply

Follow these steps to complete your application to Softcore University:

Choose Your Program

Select the program you wish to apply for from our list of undergraduate, postgraduate, or diploma programs.

Check Requirements

Ensure you meet all the admission requirements for your chosen program.

Complete Application Form

Fill out the online application form or download and complete the PDF version.

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents including academic certificates, ID, and passport photos.

Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee through the provided payment methods.

Submit Application

Submit your completed application form and all supporting documents to the Admissions Office.

Application Review

Wait for the Admissions Committee to review your application (typically 2-4 weeks).

Receive Admission Letter

Successful applicants will receive an official admission letter with registration details.

Begin Online Application

Or download the application form to apply offline
