Prof. Jane W. Muthoni

Vice Chancellor, Softcore University

Professor Jane W. Muthoni is the 5th Vice Chancellor of Softcore University, appointed in 2020. A distinguished scholar in Electrical Engineering, she holds a PhD from the University of Nairobi and has published extensively in renewable energy systems. Prior to her current role, she served as Deputy Vice Chancellor (Academic Affairs) at TechBridge University.

About the VC's Office

The Office of the Vice Chancellor provides strategic leadership and direction for the university. The VC is responsible for the overall administration of the university and the implementation of policies and decisions of the University Council.

Key functions include academic leadership, financial management, human resource development, and maintaining relationships with government, industry and international partners.
