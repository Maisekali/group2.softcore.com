About the Faculty

Established in 1995, the Faculty of Medicine at Softcore University has grown to become one of the leading medical schools in the region. We offer a comprehensive range of programs from undergraduate to doctoral levels, all designed to produce skilled healthcare professionals.

Our faculty comprises experienced medical practitioners and researchers who are committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community service. We have partnerships with leading hospitals and research institutions both locally and internationally.

Departments

Department of Human Anatomy

Focuses on the structure of the human body through gross anatomy, histology, and embryology.

Department of Physiology

Studies the functions and mechanisms of the human body systems.

Department of Biochemistry

Examines the chemical processes within and related to living organisms.

Department of Pathology

Studies the causes and effects of diseases and injuries.

Department of Pharmacology

Focuses on drug action and the development of new medications.

Department of Community Health

Addresses health issues at the population level through prevention and education.

Department of Surgery

Trains students in operative procedures and surgical techniques.

Department of Internal Medicine

Focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases.

Department of Pediatrics

Specializes in the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents.

Department of Obstetrics & Gynecology

Focuses on women's health, pregnancy, and childbirth.
